About Our School

Pyrotechnic University of Pasig is a premier educational institution dedicated to nurturing young minds and preparing them for the challenges of creating fireworks. With a rich history spanning over three days, we take pride in our tradition of academic excellence and holistic development.

Our state-of-the-art facilities, experienced faculty, and innovative curriculum create an environment where students can thrive academically, socially, and emotionally.
